DOI Chronograph

The data here is only a small demo sample. Do not use this data for anything yet.

Lots of things can happen to a DOI at the start of its lifetime. The DOI Chronograph tries to capture some of it.

Currently being collected, in progress, or planned:

  • date publication was issued according to publisher
  • first time a publisher deposited metadata to CrossRef (both in the CrossRef schema format and a real ISO date)
  • most recent time a publisher deposited metadata to CrossRef
  • date the DOI was first clicked / resolved (by a human or otherwise)
  • date on which it was first possible to follow the DOI
  • URL the DOI resolved (both initial redirect and ultimate redirect) to the first time it was possible to follow it

This project is in its early stages, and still collecting data. Contact jwass@crossref.org for information.
