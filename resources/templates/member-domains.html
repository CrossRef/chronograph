CrossRef Member domains

The statistics for domains belonging to for CrossRef members (mostly publishers) is not available to the public.

Because of the central and unique role that DOIs play in scholarly publishing, this would include a large amount of traffic between, and within publishers' websites. This traffic may be commercially sensitive and we can't release it.

If you are on this list and want your data to be public, or have any queries about this list please email jwass@crossref.org
